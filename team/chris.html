I'm Christopher Baumann. I first was introduced to coding in 7th grade when I did some Python in a math class. The teacher gave us a file with a bunch of PDF's outlining the lessons for the duration of the unit, and I went home and went through the whole thing that night. Since then I knew I wanted to do something with programming for a job. During high school I also started a Youtube channel (which you will never find) where as part of the videos I made a simple 3-d animation for my intro and a different one each week for my outro. This also got me into animation and a little bit of modelling. Finally senior year I did the artistic project in Human Bio by making a simple game in Unity using C# from scratch detailing the path of a virus through the human body. Since then I've kept making games and programming, learning about C# mostly, while still playing plenty of games myself.

The game I've probably played the most of is League of Legends, but the games I play rotate depending on what I find most interesting at the time. I also like making Origami, collecting knives, and being able to talk for hours about the lore of games I have never played.
