Code of Ethics:
1. Do your best to always tell the truth.
2. Follow the scout oath.
3. Be willing to do something for anyone if they ask me to. As long as it's outside of their ability and I don't feel like they're using me.
4. Do unto others as you'd have them do unto you.
5. When life gets boring, look at things from a different perspective. The same goes if I'm not the person I want to be.
6. I feel like it's best to stay out of other people's problems, because in the past i feel like some people have become reliant on me for doing so.
7. I do my best to enable and to teach others when possible and acceptable.
